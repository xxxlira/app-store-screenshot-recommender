# Duolingo-style Playful

- Category: 教育 / 知识
- Style tags: playful, bold, 3d
- Source: App Store US
- Platform: iOS

## Visual style
- Composition: bright flat color block background, a friendly 3D mascot or illustration
  front-and-center, big rounded headline.
- Color: warm cream `#FFF4E6` or vivid green `#58CC02` dominant, high-saturation accent
  for highlights; energetic, not subtle.
- Typography: rounded, chunky display face (e.g. `Baloo 2`, `Fredoka`, `Poppins` bold),
  large and bubbly; body in a clean sans.
- Mood: fun, encouraging, low-pressure.

## Structure of the promo
- Lead message: a playful challenge or reward ("5 minutes a day → fluent!").
- Supporting elements: mascot/illustration, progress badge or streak counter as social
  proof.

## Why it works
- Playful visuals lower the intimidation of learning; the mascot creates attachment.
- Bold color + rounded type reads as "safe to try", driving installs.

## Reusable patterns
- Use a mascot or character as the emotional hook.
- High-saturation single-hue background + one contrasting accent.
- Rounded, heavy display type for headlines aimed at casual/young audiences.
