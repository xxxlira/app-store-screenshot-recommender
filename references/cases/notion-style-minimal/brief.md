# Notion-style Minimal

- Category: 效率工具
- Style tags: minimal, light, editorial
- Source: App Store CN
- Platform: iOS

## Visual style
- Composition: generous whitespace, single centered headline, one small product UI
  snippet below; no device frame.
- Color: near-white background `#FFFFFF`, ink text `#1D1D1F`, single blue accent
  `#0071E3` used only on the CTA.
- Typography: `SF Pro Display` / `Inter` for headline (semibold, large), `Inter` for
  body; Chinese fallback `Noto Sans SC`.
- Mood: calm, professional, trustworthy.

## Structure of the promo
- Lead message: a single benefit statement ("Write, plan, organize — in one workspace").
- Supporting elements: one cropped UI card, no phone bezel.

## Why it works
- Whitespace signals confidence and premium quality; the eye lands on one message.
- Restrained accent color makes the CTA the only "loud" element.

## Reusable patterns
- 60-30-10 with a near-neutral base and one accent.
- One focal point per screenshot; let emptiness do the work.
- Pair a neutral body font with a slightly characterful display face.
